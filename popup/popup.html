Prompt Engineering Tips

Be Direct

Skip pleasantries. "Write a poem about spring" works as well as "Could you please write me a poem about spring?"

Remove Self-References

Instead of "I want you to explain...", just say "Explain..."

Avoid Hedging

Words like "maybe", "perhaps", "possibly" weaken your prompt. Be confident.

Use Clear Structure

Format: [Task]: [Details]. Example: "Summarize: The history of Rome in 3 paragraphs"

Condense Phrases

"in order to" → "to", "due to the fact that" → "because", "at this point in time" → "now"
